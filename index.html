Bio:

I'm a teen who loves to read and write. My love of writing began when I first read the Hunger Games series my freshman year of highschool. The way she wrote made me believe I could change the world with writing. Even though I was inspired, I never got serious about writing until my second year in high school. I finished my first novel at in a year at 16, just as I was going into my junior year of highschool. I'm planning to write many more. Going into my senior year of high school, I can’t wait for graduation, and what my future will hold.
